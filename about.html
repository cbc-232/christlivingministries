Our Belief

We believe in the limitless and transformative power of faith in Jesus Christ as the cornerstone of healing, restoration, and growth. Through unwavering belief, individuals are set free from past burdens, families are strengthened, and communities are uplifted. We hold that true transformation begins in the heart, and through the living Word, broken lives are made whole, destinies are realigned, and hope is reignited in even the darkest places.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a global beacon of hope and transformation — a house of light shining across nations, cultures, and generations. We envision a world where faith unites communities, where the love of Christ breaks through barriers of fear and injustice, and where every soul encounters purpose and belonging. By living out our calling with courage and compassion, we aim to ignite revival, nurture leaders, and leave a legacy of faith-driven change that echoes for generations.

Our Mission

Our mission is clear: to restore lost souls, empower lives burdened by adversity, and build communities anchored in faith, love, and resilience. We are called to reach the forgotten, the marginalized, and the hungry — spiritually, emotionally, and physically — by spreading the teachings of Christ and demonstrating His love through action. Through worship, outreach, mentorship, and service, we strive to be the hands and feet of Jesus, building bridges of hope where there were once walls of despair.

Our Core Values

Faith: Rooted in the unshakeable promises of God, our faith moves mountains and inspires action.
Integrity: We uphold honesty, accountability, and righteousness in every aspect of our ministry and lives.
Compassion: Reflecting Christ’s heart, we extend grace and kindness to the hurting and the lost.
Empowerment: We nurture gifts, champion dreams, and raise leaders who will transform their spheres of influence.
Community: We believe in the power of unity — building strong, loving communities that reflect the Kingdom of God on earth.

Have Questions? We Have Answers.

Explore more about Christ Living Ministries Int’l — our mission, programs, and how you can get involved.

What is the heart of Christ Living Ministries Int’l Church?

We are a Christ-centered community committed to restoring souls, empowering lives, and building resilient communities through faith, charity, and unity.

What programs and ministries do you offer?

Our ministries include Worship Services, Youth Empowerment, Women and Men Fellowships, Community Outreach through our Foundation, and Youth Sports Development via our Football Program.

How can I become a member or join the ministry?

Joining is simple! Attend one of our services, connect with a welcome team member, or sign up through our website to learn more about baptism, membership classes, and ministry involvement.

How do I volunteer or get involved?

We welcome volunteers in ministries, outreach programs, and special events. Fill out our volunteer interest form online or speak to a team leader after service to get started!

What are your service times and locations?

We hold weekly Sunday Worship at 10:00 AM and midweek services every Wednesday at 6:00 PM. Our main location is 15c Wilberforce Loop, Pipe Line, Freetown, Sierra Leone.

How can I contribute to the mission financially?

You can support us through tithes, offerings, or special project donations via mobile money, bank transfers, or online giving platforms listed on our Donate page.

Who leads Christ Living Ministries Int’l Church?

Our ministry is led by Rev. Joseph Nabieu, Pastor Mary Nabieu, and a team of dedicated spiritual and community leaders committed to guiding with love, vision, and integrity.

Be part of Christ Living Ministries Int'l Church’s mission to uplift, empower, and spread love. Your involvement can make a lasting impact in our community and beyond.
