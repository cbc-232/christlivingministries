Our Ministries

Transforming lives through faith, compassion, and empowerment.

Christ Living Ministries — A Place of Transformation

At Christ Living Ministries Int’l Church, we are more than a congregation — we are a family of believers pursuing deeper intimacy with Christ. Through vibrant worship, sound biblical teaching, and compassionate fellowship, we empower individuals to walk boldly in their faith journey, discover their God-given purpose, and impact the world around them. Our church stands as a spiritual lighthouse — restoring souls, nurturing spiritual gifts, and building a legacy of faith that transcends generations.

  • Inspiring sermons and biblical teachings.
  • Engaging worship services and prayer meetings.
  • Spiritual counseling and life mentorship.

Christ Living Foundation — Touching Lives, Building Futures

The Christ Living Foundation embodies our mission to serve the least, the lost, and the hurting with tangible acts of love. Through education support, health initiatives, food programs, and sustainable community projects, we uplift lives and open doors of opportunity. We believe that lasting transformation happens when faith meets action — building stronger families, empowered youth, and thriving communities anchored in hope and dignity.

  • Food, shelter, and healthcare for the needy.
  • Educational support and scholarships.
  • Community-driven charity events and projects.

Empowering Youth Through Sports

Spring Of Life FootBall Club — Building Champions Beyond the Field

At the Spring Of Life FootBall Club, sports become a platform for life transformation. Through disciplined training, mentorship, teamwork, and leadership development, we nurture not just skilled athletes but future leaders with character, vision, and resilience. Our program teaches young people to dream big, work hard, honor God, and serve their communities — preparing them to win both on and off the field.

  • Skill-focused training and physical fitness development.
  • Mentorship and personal growth programs.
  • Competitive matches inspiring excellence and pride.
