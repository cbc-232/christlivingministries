Have a question? Check out our FAQs

Get quick answers about our ministries, foundation, football program, and donation opportunities.

What programs does Christ Living Ministries International Church offer?

We're dedicated to a variety of missions: our Ministry serves spiritual needs, our Foundation supports community welfare, our Football Program engages youth, and we have multiple donation channels to support these causes.

How can I reach out for support or more information?

Feel free to contact us via our online form, email, or directly by phone. Our team is ready to assist with any inquiries about our programs and initiatives.

What is the process for joining one of your programs?

We make joining simple and welcoming. From the start, we guide each individual to ensure they receive the support and resources they need to make the most of their involvement.

Can I customize my contributions or donations?

Yes! We understand each contributor's goals may differ, so we offer options to direct your donations towards the Ministry, Foundation, Football Program, or any specific project that aligns with your mission.

What are the ways I can contribute or make donations?

We accept various types of donations, including online payments, bank transfers, and in-person gifts. Flexible contribution plans are also available to support ongoing involvement.

Do you offer continued guidance or support for contributors?

Absolutely. Our team provides ongoing updates and support to keep contributors informed and connected with the impact their contributions are making.
