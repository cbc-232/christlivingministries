All Branches

Prayer Rain

14th-16th Every Month
Full Time - 3 Days Complete
Central Church

Join us for the Prayer Rain, a 3-day spiritual encounter held from the 14th to the 16th of every month at the Central Church. This full-time prayer event is dedicated to seeking God's face for breakthroughs, healing, and divine direction. Come and be part of a transformative experience.

Community Outreach & Charity Drive

August 5-7, 2025
10:00 AM - 03:00 PM
Freetown City

Extending the love of Christ to the less privileged through food distribution, medical aid, and skill-building workshops. Be part of a movement that brings hope, healing, and tangible support to those in need. Together, we can make a difference!

Youth Revival & Worship Night

July 22, 2025
07:00 PM - 10:00 PM
Freetown City

A night of radical worship, divine impartation, and prophetic encounters for the next generation. Young believers will be ignited to live boldly for Christ, walk in their purpose, and build a strong community of faith-driven leaders.

Bible Mastery Bootcamp

August 5-7, 2025

Church Compound

Holy Spirit Encounter & Anointing Service
